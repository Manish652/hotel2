About Us

Tasty Bites, located in Contai, West Bengal, is a delightful restaurant founded by Arjun and Meera Patel. It began as a small dream to bring authentic Indian flavors to their community. The restaurant's charming decor features traditional Indian artwork, creating a warm and inviting atmosphere. Known for its delicious Bengali Fish Curry, Chicken Korma, and Vegetable Biryani, Tasty Bites offers a true taste of regional cuisine. It's not just a place to eat but a vibrant community hub, hosting local events and cooking classes. Committed to sustainability, Tasty Bites uses local ingredients and eco-friendly practices, making it a cherished spot in Contai.
